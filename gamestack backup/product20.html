League Of Legends

Release Date : October 27, 2009

Type : Multiplayer

C$ 19.99

Description

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game in the three-dimensional isometric perspective. The game consists of two current running game modes: Summoner's Rift and Howling Abyss. Players compete in matches, lasting anywhere from 20 to 50 minutes on average. In each game mode, teams work together to achieve a victory condition, typically destroying the core building (called the Nexus) in the enemy team's base after bypassing a line of defensive structures called turrets, or towers. In all game modes, players control characters called champions, chosen or assigned every match, who each have a set of unique abilities. Champions begin every match at a low level, and then gain experience over the course of the match to achieve a maximum level of 18.
