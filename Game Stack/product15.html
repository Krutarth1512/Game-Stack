The Last Of Us 2

Release Date : June 19, 2020

Type : Action-adventure
      Survival horror

C$ 99.99

Description

The Last of Us Part II is an upcoming action-adventure survival horror game played from the third-person perspective.[1][2] Players can use firearms, improvised weapons, and stealth to defend against hostile humans and cannibalistic creatures infected by a mutated strain of the Cordyceps fungus. In it the player character can traverse the environment further by being able to reach higher vantage points by jumping and climbing whilst playing as the more nimble Ellie. Players can also crawl in a prone position to evade enemies. Throughout the game, players will have an non-player character assisting them.
