Anno 1800

Release Date : April 16, 2019

Type : City-building, Real-time strategy

C$ 30.00

Description

Anno 1800 is a city-building and strategy game.The Anno 1800 is set in the Old World, where the needs of the citizens, workers and artisans are central to the management of production and supply chains. A parallel New World city exists, which produces products that laborers in the Old World want to purchase, thus trade routes need to be established.
